Peel's First Online Hackathon

Introducing Mississauga Hacks, the first ever online Hackathon in Peel. Because of the COVID-19 situation this year, Mississauga SS has decided to organize an online hackathon for all Peel students. This hackathon will give students the oppurtunity to create amazing things and use their submissions to compete with others. All participants will be put on showcase, and from there the voting stage will begin. A handful of winners will be selected, and monetary prizes will be distributed among them. Lets Begin!
